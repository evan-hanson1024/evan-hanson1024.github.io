Academic Projects

Numerical Tic-Tac-Toe AI

Designed and built an AI to play the game of numerical tic-tac-toe against a human or another AI. This AI was designed using a Monte Carlo simulation to analyze every possible game state and determine which move would give the AI the most probable chance of winning. Because this AI was designed to play perfectly, it is unable to lose and will only, at worst, draw.
